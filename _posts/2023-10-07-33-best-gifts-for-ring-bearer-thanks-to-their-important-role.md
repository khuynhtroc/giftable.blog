A ring bearer, also known as a pageboy, is a boy assigned to act as a "ring guardian". This is the person who will hold a small pillow or ring box with a ring on it and enter the aisle. The boy will then give a ring to the **[groom](https://avada.io/loveable/cool-groomsmen-gifts/)**. Those little angels are critical characters at the wedding. The presence of the ring boy in the moment of solemn ring exchange not only helps the bride and groom reduce stress but also creates a joyful atmosphere for the whole party.

Don’t hesitate to give this little man a funny and useful gift that reminds him of his important role in the wedding and makes him feel extra special and cool as being a ring bearer thanks to different sparkling accessories. This is also your uttermost thanks and gratitude to this little boy.

Our suggestion includes various items suitable for all ages, from toys, **[personalized water bottles](https://avada.io/loveable/personalized-water-bottles/)**, to sunglasses and books that he can use on his special day or use every day. In addition, a thoughtful gift like a small toy or **[personalized children's books](https://avada.io/loveable/personalized-children-books/)** is ideal because he will not only be excited to receive a new toy, he'll have something to do during the reception to develop their brain and foster their creativity. Let's create hours of fun and creativity for the ring bearer to get him excited for the wedding.
