Written by [Benjamin Collins](https://avada.io/loveable/author/benjamin/) Last Updated on July 25, 2023

Researched by [David Flynn](https://avada.io/loveable/author/david-flynn/) FROM LOVEABLE LAB

Christmas is a special time to show your loved ones how much you care. For many people, that includes their **[godparents](https://avada.io/loveable/gifts-for-godmothers-from-godchild/)**. If you're seeking a perfect gift to give your godparents this holiday season, here are some ideas to get you started.

One thoughtful gift idea is to put together a basket of their favorite things. This could include items like food, drink, or other treats. You could also include a **[personalized mug](https://avada.io/loveable/mugs-gifts/)** or ornament. Another option is to give a gift that will be useful to them. This can be something like a gift card to their favorite restaurant or store. Alternatively, you could get them a subscription to a magazine or online service that they love.

Consider something that reflects their interests or hobbies if you want to pick a special gift. For example, if they **[love to travel](https://avada.io/loveable/gifts-travelers/)**, you could get them a map of the world or a guidebook to their favorite destination. Or, if they're avid readers, you could get them a new book by their favorite author. Whatever you decide to give your godparents this Christmas, make sure it's something that comes from the heart. They'll appreciate the thoughtfulness and love that went into choosing the perfect gift for them.
