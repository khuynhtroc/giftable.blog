## **8 Christmas Lights for Office Decoration**

### **1\. Snow Globe Terrariums**

![Snow Globe Terrariums](images/snow-globe-terrariums.jpg "snow-globe-terrariums")

You can easily create these charming DIY winter-themed terrariums in just 10 minutes. Grab a couple of apothecary jars and fill them with imitation snow, LED lights, classic figurines, and bottlebrush trees.

Get the tutorial at [Pink Pistachio](http://www.pinkpistachio.com/let-it-snow/)

### **2\. DIY Copper Lighted Wreaths**

![DIY Copper Lighted Wreaths](images/diy-copper-lighted-wreaths.jpg "diy-copper-lighted-wreaths")

This stylish ornament resembles a minimalist Christmas wreath. Shape copper coils into spheres and then envelop them with coordinating copper string firefly lights. Experiment with different sizes to achieve a charmingly uneven arrangement that radiates a warm glow.

Get the tutorial at [A Bubbly Life](http://www.abubblylife.com/2015/11/diy-copper-lighted-wreaths.html)

### **3\. Faux Metal Star Lantern**

![Faux Metal Star Lantern](images/faux-metal-star-lantern.jpg "faux-metal-star-lantern")

This lantern is ingeniously crafted from cut cardstock—a truly creative endeavor! Blogger Ananda generously provides stencil templates and a rust pattern for free, allowing you to fashion your very own celestial faux metal lamp, illuminated by twinkling Christmas lights.

Get the tutorial at [A Piece of Rainbow](http://www.apieceofrainbow.com/make-star-lantern/)

### **4\. Twine Spheres**

![Twine Spheres](images/twine-spheres.jpg "twine-spheres")

There’s no requirement to purchase those twine balls found in craft shops—crafting your own is simple, using balloons, twine, and Mod Podge. Once completed, insert a strand of lights, and you’ll have a magnificent holiday ornament ready to grace your office space.

Get the tutorial at [Mucho Crafts](http://www.muchocrafts.com/2014/12/twine-spheres-diy.html#more)

### **5\. Hallway Twinkle Lights**

![Hallway Twinkle Lights](images/hallway-twinkle-lights.jpg "hallway-twinkle-lights")

This simple yet captivating decor idea has the power to completely transform the atmosphere of your office hallway. The soft and twinkling illumination provided by the lights creates a warm and inviting ambiance that is sure to enchant anyone who passes through. 

Get the tutorial at [Nest of Posies](http://www.nestofposies-blog.com/2012/12/all-things-merry-bright-add-twinkle-lights-to-your-hallway/)

### **6\. Stars Garland Christmas Mantel**

![Stars Garland Christmas Mantel](images/stars-garland-christmas-mantel.jpg "stars-garland-christmas-mantel")

These stars can be thoughtfully sewn onto fairy lights, creating a captivating visual spectacle that evokes the magic of the season. As the fairy lights twinkle within your reclaimed wood village, the stars will cast a warm and inviting glow, turning your creation into a mesmerizing centerpiece.

Get the tutorial at [Song Bird Blog](http://www.songbirdblog.com/garlands-stars-houses-christmas-mantel/)

### **7\. Fairy Light Jars**

![Fairy Light Jars](images/fairy-light-jars.jpg "fairy-light-jars")

Transforming a beloved rural classic into a holiday delight, this DIY endeavor takes center stage: Just pack a Mason jar with an array of string lights, and then gather them in clusters on a mantel or entryway table.

Get the tutorial at [Making Lemonade](http://makinglemonadeblog.com/holiday-party-tips-ideas-decor/)

### **8\. DIY Flameless Fire Pit**

![DIY Flameless Fire Pit](images/diy-flameless-fire-pit.jpg "diy-flameless-fire-pit")

When the charm of a fireplace is absent from your living space, fear not—there’s a delightful solution to infuse an extra touch of “warmth” into your working area. This creative DIY project brings forth an adorable faux fire pit that not only adds visual appeal but also creates a comforting ambiance reminiscent of a crackling fire.

Get the tutorial at [Free People](http://blog.freepeople.com/2012/11/diy-flameless-fire-pit/)
