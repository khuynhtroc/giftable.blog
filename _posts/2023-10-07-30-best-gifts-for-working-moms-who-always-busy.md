Some mothers run marathons, some operate businesses, and still, others run around town transporting their children to violin lessons, tae-kwon-do classes, and softball practice. As moms, they spend a lot of time shopping for others and don't have time to treat themselves since it doesn't seem feasible. Or they are too busy to consider taking some "Me Time" and are continually falling behind the rest of the family. However, these women all share one trait: they all have at least one person (whether it a child, a co-parent, or an admirer) who owes them a heartfelt thank-you, accompanied by a thoughtful gift, for everything they do.
