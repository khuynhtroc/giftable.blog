Whether you're looking for interesting beer gifts for your father, **[stepfather](/fathers-day-gifts-step-dad/)**, or father-in-law, finding a delicious craft beer gift that he'll truly love is no easy chore. This Father's Day, you can't go wrong with a six-pack of his favorite brews like Bud, Corona, and other insipid macro beers. However, the likelihood is that it will be gone before the week is through, and sometimes you want to offer a present that will last (or that you can easily mail to him if you aren't seeing him in person, as opposed to a bottle or two of beer).

Besides, unless you are intimately familiar with your father's favorite beers and types, it is definitely better to avoid single beers. Beer enthusiasts are, of course, extremely picky about the beers they consume. They may be picky about their hops and have strong feelings about the latest beer trend, whether positive or negative. Rather than that, give him a present of actual quality, character, and value.
