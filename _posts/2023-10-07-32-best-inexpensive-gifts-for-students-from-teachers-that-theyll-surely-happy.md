# 32 Best Inexpensive Gifts For Students From Teachers That They'll Surely Happy

Written by [Alex Smith](https://avada.io/loveable/author/alex/) Last Updated on August 18, 2023

Researched by [Linda Hill](https://avada.io/loveable/author/linda-hill/) FROM LOVEABLE LAB

Teachers are typically seen as authority figures by their students. This means that students often respect and look up to their teachers. Additionally, because teachers play such an important role in their students' lives, students often form strong bonds with their teachers. Teachers give gifts to students to commemorate special events like **[birthdays](https://avada.io/loveable/birthday/)**, holidays, or the end of the school year. A small, inexpensive gift is often more appreciated than a large, expensive one.

There are a few things to consider when choosing inexpensive gifts for students from teachers. First, think about what the student might actually use or appreciate. A gift that is unnecessary or does not fit the student's interests is likely to be quickly forgotten. Second, try to find something that is unique and memorable; a boring or common gift is unlikely to be appreciated as much as something that is more thoughtful. With these things in mind, here are some ideas for inexpensive **[gifts for students from teachers on christmas](https://avada.io/loveable/christmas-gifts-students-teachers/)** or any occasion.
