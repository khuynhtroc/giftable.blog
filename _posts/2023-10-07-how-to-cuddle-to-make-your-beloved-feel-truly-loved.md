## **How To Cuddle To Make Your Beloved Feel Truly Loved**

### **Begin slowly**

First and foremost, you have to make sure that your partners want to have a cuddle. Let’s slowly check her behavior after you have some intimate action, like snuggling. Therefore, you must take care of your actions to ensure that she/he is always comfortable. Show your partner your intention that you want to know how to cuddle.

There are some suggestions for you. You can slowly take your hand to his/her shoulder and lightly caress it to check her reaction. If you can feel her agreement, continue to the next steps.

### **Be mindful of environmental influences**

![Be mindful of environmental influences](images/2-1.png "2-1")

So you have covered the fundamentals and are ready to go into some cuddling. However, before you make the switch, consider several essential environmental aspects. Considering the outside environment, whether it is hot or cold, or you two are on the bed or sofa it that is very important to have a good cuddle with your partner. 

### **Get Relax**

You and your lover are ready to have a long snuggling time, so prepare carefully so that the two also feel well. Besides environmental factors, you can consider some other aspects such as clothes, postures, etc. Wearing comfortable clothes supports you and your partner in intimacy. It seems to have no effect but it is really important in the process of cuddling between the two of you.

## **The best cuddling positions to try in a relationship**

### **Hands-on contact**

This is the best simple way to show off the love of the two lovers. It is just simple to approach your partner and catch his/her hand. You can consider doing that in a subtle way, like pretending to take something but suddenly catching her hand. It’s both a surprising and romantic act.

### **Facing each other while seated closely**

Eye contact is also the best way to snuggle your lover. Look directly at her/his eyes to transfer your love energy non-verbal. After that, you could do something more to enhance the burning love. This is also the first step for all, you cannot immediately jump into yours and start to cuddle like a monster. Let’s show your gentleness first to create the ensuring feeling.

![Facing each other while seated closely](images/3-4.png "3-4")

### **Lying on your lap with their head on it**

The higher level of love behaviors in how to cuddle is here. When the one put the head on your lap, generating a cozy love feeling between the two. Let’s put your hand on his/her head and slightly caress his/her hair. It will create a sense of security and a high connection between the two of you.

### **Sitting hug**

Do you know how to cuddle with your boyfriend/girlfriend effectively? Sitting by her side first and closing up all parts of the body as much as possible. When hand to hand, hip to hip, and head touching, whispering words of love to her/his ear to start cuddling. This position is considered the most common for intimating but can much more show off the sincere love for your lover.

![Sitting hug](images/4-1.png "4-1")

### **In a lounge chair**

This way is carried out by sitting and broadening your legs into a V shape to welcome your partner. Let he/she leans on your body and embrace it. Look at your partner from the back, and you will look like a gigantic guardian. This cuddling technique seems like you are a shield to protect her. So, a safe feeling to come into strong love.
