Find out whether or not the book lover in your life has a favorite author. If they do, you may want to consider getting them a book by that author. Chances are, they are able to appreciate the gesture and be more likely to read it.

Finally, you should also think about what kinds of things the book lover in your life likes to do outside of **[reading](/gifts-readers/)**. Do they enjoy spending time outdoors? If so, you may consider getting them a gift related to their interests. For example, you could get them a hiking book or map if they love **[hiking](/gifts-hikers/)**.
