### **2\. Have A People Scavenger Hunt**

![Have A People Scavenger Hunt](images/wozhmnnbitkkm_4kxlk1_akfmjyes9aeouwzd_wflww7p3c-tvieaiwao28qagmi497b3mtwg-dewj1g7g73wih2hjjyokkn1xwfr3n5bfngdfrteqrczvno7tjx3oyaaxxoi7ffdkdb8cyndajnxv4.png "wozhmnnbitkkm_4kxlk1_akfmjyes9aeouwzd_wflww7p3c-tvieaiwao28qagmi497b3mtwg-dewj1g7g73wih2hjjyokkn1xwfr3n5bfngdfrteqrczvno7tjx3oyaaxxoi7ffdkdb8cyndajnxv4")
