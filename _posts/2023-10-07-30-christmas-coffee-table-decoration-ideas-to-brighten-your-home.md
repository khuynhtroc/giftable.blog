As the holiday season comes knocking, it’s time to infuse your living space with the festive spirit. One area that often takes center stage in your home is the coffee table. Through thoughtful arrangement, you can craft captivating scenes that elevate your coffee table into the main attraction of your living area. In this article, we bring you a collection of stunning **Christmas coffee table decor ideas** that will have your guests in awe.
