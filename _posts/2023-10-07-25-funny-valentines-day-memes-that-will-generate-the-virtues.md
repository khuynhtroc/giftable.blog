Are you fortunate enough to be able to enjoy [Valentine’s Day](https://avada.io/loveable/what-is-valentines-day/) with true love? If the answer is not, sadly, but instead of wailing in a corner over being perpetually alone, you can find alternative activities to keep yourself active or engaged. While most couples spend Valentine’s Day at trendy discos, bars, or exotic locales, singles typically spend time for themselves to enjoy isolation. A suggestion for you to immerse yourself in entertainment is collecting Valentine’s day memes. 

As we see on social media platforms, meme photos are no longer strange to us. It is common to catch funny memes when we are surfing Facebook, Instagram, Twitter or TikTok, etc. People often save them to send for close relationships to make laugh. So what do memes actually mean? Fundamentally, a meme is an idea, image, or video that is extended very quickly on the internet containing humorous elements. They have been especially popular and welcomed by teenagers since the 90s so far have become indispensable spiritual food for young people.

Don’t be depressed anymore; whether you are single or not, it doesn’t matter. We have a celebration, not for sadness. This article provides some [funny Valentine’s](https://avada.io/loveable/funny-valentines-gifts/) day memes, then let’s save all these memes to share with anybody. What makes you and them can’t stop laughing?
