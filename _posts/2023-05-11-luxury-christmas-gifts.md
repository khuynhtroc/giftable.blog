Traditionally, Christmas gifts have been cheaper than other presents given as an expression of love or friendship. This is because the gift should be something anyone could use to celebrate the holiday season with their loved ones. In this case, replacing a traditional Christmas gift with a luxury one isn’t necessarily wrong. However, you should choose something expensive enough that your friend won’t mind parting with their old gift for it.

Luxury Christmas gifts don’t have to be expensive either— they can be quite affordable if the right thoughtfulness goes into choosing them. If you are planning on **[buying your friend](/best-friend-christmas-gifts/)** a costly luxury Christmas gift, do your research so you know exactly how much they would appreciate your thoughtfulness!

A traditional Christmas gift should not be replaced by a new one— showing appreciation for someone’s wealth doesn’t mean they have to miss out on their **[old favorite toy](/1970s-toys/)**! Instead, consider choosing a luxurious Christmas gift to show your friend how much you appreciate their abundance. Or, if you feel like spending more money than usual, consider gifting them with tickets to an extravagant event instead! Whatever type of luxury Christmas gift you choose, make sure it is thoughtful and appropriate for the recipient before setting it out!
